Chilean Saints

How many Chilean Saints are there?

Currently there are only 2 Saints which are:

1.- Santa Teresa

2.- San Alberto Hurtado


What meaning do they have?

In the Christian tradition, these are people noted for their virtues and

They are like models capable of showing others an exemplary path of perfection.

Since, according to the Bible, God is love, the main virtue of the saints is,

consequently, their capacity to love God and other human beings
